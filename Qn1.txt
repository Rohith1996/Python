Following are some of operational procedures if carried can reduce the risks of damages caused .

* Safety Work representative must be appointed so that he can analyse the problems occurring in the community of workers and intimate his higher officials to take necessary actions upon them .

* Frequent meeting to be conducted weekly so as to validate and check the safety and health status of employees .

* Complaints should be filed if any accidents occurred so that it can useful to analyse commonly occurring accidents and provide a solution for it .
